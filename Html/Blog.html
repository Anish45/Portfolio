Technology in Household

There is a term 'Smart house' which makes us wow when we hear the term. The concept of the smart home shows us the innovation of the modern technology. All the devices in the huse are connected in a Lan and the sensors and many appliances are connected to the internet of things which can be remotely monitored, controlled or accessed. When the smart home company Insteon introduced technology that combined electric wiring with wireless signal. If we have a smart Tv in our house then we can access to the internet and surf the internet on the TV. We can place a fingerprint scanner in the doors of our house and smart cameras for face recognization which are for security purposes. Due to the technology in the house it will be very easy for us human beings in our daily household activites which is considered as a gratest innovation in the field of technology.

We are using technologies like projectors in the class so that the knowledge shared might be effective and the students dont get bored and dont waste their time by writing notes. We can search or ask the teachers anytime when we want with these modern technologies. We dont need to buy books which costs money. So by applying technologies we can provide the young generation a very good quality of education which cause in bringing greater and new innovations.

Technology as Addiction and Waste of Time

Although the technology has helped us in many ways it is considered as a waste of time. We need to use the technology but not make that technology as a addiction. There are so many things in the internet which is not possible to surf it all. So, we must use it wisely. unfortunately, many of us are using technologies as a addiction which is making our time waste. So, it is sometimes better to spend time in doing some practical works and walking into the nature or going away from gadgets for some time as a refreshment. We are now only dependent upon the technology which is making us addicted to it. Now almost no one can live without these gadgets so instead of we using our gadgets they are using us. Due to addiction they will be looking only the screens of gadgets which makes them Stress and many of them make harm in their studies. Too much addiction can cause in poor sleep by surfing the internet at late nights.

Technology as Threat to Health and Privacy

The more time people are spending looking at their computer screens, playing games, surfing social medias they are spending less time in exercises and being less active. This can cause in increased consumption of unhealthy food, depression and obesity. Also, there is a huge possibility of loss of eyesight and hearing who uses headphones. Constantly looking at the screen may cause head nad neck pain. Internet and many social networking sites encourage people to be public about their personal life. People are sharing their personal lifes in social medias through the posts. It is estimated that the problem with lack of privacy will only get worse with further development of the technology. Due to modern technologies it is affecing our brains. After spending on the internet, we found that it is hard to read books and make us lose the ability to work on something with more focus.
